BACKGROUND

KEHINDE WILEY IS A NIGERIAN-AMERICAN ARTIST BEST KNOWN FOR HIS PORTRAITS AND SCULPTURES THAT FEATURE AFRICAN-AMERICAN SUBJECTS IN UNEXPECTED SCENES. HE IS BASED IN BROOKLYN BUT HIS ART HAS MADE ITS WAY INTO MUSEUMS AND MEDIA THROUGHOUT THE WORLD. HIS WORK CHALLENGES PERCEPTIONS OF BLACKNESS, MASCULINITY, SEXUALITY, AND MUCH MORE.
